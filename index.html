Sql to VBA formatter tool.

This is a free tool that will be very useful when you need to automatizate your SQL queries using only Excel (with VBA, of course).

Using it is very very simple. You only have to:

1 - Copy your query and paste in the first box;

2 - Click on the format button;

3 - Click on the copy button;

4 - Enjoy your formatted query and share this tool with your friends ;)
